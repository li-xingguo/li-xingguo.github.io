Xingguo Li

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the Computer Science Department at Princeton University, working with Prof. Sanjeev Arora. Before that, I received my Ph.D. in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Minnesota, under the supervision of Prof. Jarvis Haupt.

Here is my CV and Google Scholar profile.

Research Interest

My research interests generally include machine learning, deep learning, and convex/non-convex optimization, with application in computer vision, sensing, data science, and open source development.
